--- title: The Squid author: Sarah McCann previous: /issues/volume-two/volume-2-issue-1/untitled-selection/ next: /issues/volume-two/volume-2-issue-1/the-scaffolds-up-the-building/ --- {% include sqs-poem-begin.html %} The squid alternately hides and makes herself known: she draws a shawl into the sea, lulls in the funeral. In time, with a small tilt, she propels away from the weeping lace: it is night, even in the deep. She lets out her swept hem of long reds to flirt in the downwelling starlight. {% include sqs-poem-end.html %}
